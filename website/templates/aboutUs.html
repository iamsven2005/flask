About Agegracefully
Agegracefully was founded in 2017 as a social enterprise to develop and market innovative eldercare lifestyle products that enable the elderly to live safely and age gracefully.

We offer a range of award-winning Smart Walking Sticks that look great and have many innovative safety features. They come with LED lights, auto fall alarms, radio and MP3 player, Carbon Fiber, Built-in chair, built-in umbrella and interchangeable quad base. Our wide collection provides a range of features and price points for our customers to choose from.

We have won prestigious local and international awards such as Eldercare Innovation Award 2018, Singapore Good Design Award 2019, German Design Award 2020 and Spirit of Enterprise Award 2021.
Agegracefully products are available in most of the hospital and rehab stores in Singapore, as well as on major online platforms such as Lazada, Shopee and Amazon.
To-date, more than 9,000 elderly have benefited from our Smart Walking Sticks. We have also exported overseas with distribution in Malaysia, Indonesia, Hong Kong and Japan.
As the population continues to age, there is a growing demand for innovative design in eldercare lifestyle products. This puts us in a good position to achieve our goal of being financially successful while helping the elderly age gracefully.

Frequently Ask Questions


What is a walking stick used for?

A walking stick, also known as a cane, is a portable support used by people who have difficulty walking or who need additional stability when standing or walking. Walking sticks can be used for a variety of reasons, including to help with balance, to alleviate pain or discomfort in the legs or back, or to provide support after an injury or surgery.

How do I choose the right size walking stick?

The correct size of a walking stick is typically determined by the user's height. To choose the right size, you can measure the distance from your wrist to the ground and then subtract about 3 inches. This measurement will give you the approximate length of the walking stick you should use. Alternatively, you can use a walking stick that can be adjusted to the correct height for your needs.

Can I use a walking stick for hiking or other outdoor activities?

While walking sticks can be used for a variety of activities, they are not designed for strenuous outdoor activities such as hiking or rock climbing. For these activities, you may want to consider using a trekking pole or other specialized support.

What types of walking sticks are available?

There are several types of walking sticks available, including straight sticks, folding sticks, and quad sticks (which have four feet). Walking sticks can also be made from different materials, such as wood, metal, or composite materials. Some walking sticks also have features such as built-in seats, handgrips, or reflectors.

How do I care for my walking stick?

To care for your walking stick, you should store it in a dry place when not in use to prevent it from getting damaged or wet. You should also inspect the stick regularly to ensure that it is in good working condition, and replace it if necessary. If the stick becomes dirty, you can clean it with a damp cloth or a mild soap and water solution.
